About Us

Learn.io publishes e-learning content in the form of informational posts, analytical articles, reviews, and online course round-ups.

Our mission is to create a community of online students and educators where all parties involved in e-learning can share their experiences, make better-informed purchasing decisions, and contribute towards making online education a greater force for good in the world. We hope to fulfill this mission by providing journalism that is trustworthy, authoritative, reliable, and unbiased.

Methodology

E-Leaning derives its authority from its expert authors who have deep knowledge of technology and education. Our credibility is further secured by our comprehensive review methodology that evaluates various key features of e-learning products such as the price-to-quality ratio, comprehensiveness, user-friendliness, online reputation, transparency, retention rates, and more. Our tried-and-tested review methodology ensures that all E-Student publications stay consistent despite our affiliate partnerships with various online education providers.
